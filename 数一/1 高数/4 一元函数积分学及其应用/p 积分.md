> [!tip]+ 常用结论 $(a>0)$
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> &①\int_{0}^{a}\frac{1}{x^{p}}dx\left\{\begin{array}{cc}\text{收敛,}&0<p<1\\\text{发散,}&p\geq1\end{array}\right.,\\
> &②\int_{a}^{+\infty}\frac{1}{x^{p}}dx\left\{\begin{array}{cc}\text{收敛,}&p>1\\\text{发散,}&p\leq1\end{array}\right.
> \end{aligned}$$

> [!success]+ 常用结论
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> &①\int_{0}^{a} \frac{1}{x^\alpha \ln^\beta x} \,\mathrm{d}x 
> \begin{cases} 
> \text{收敛}, & \alpha < 1 \\[4pt] 
> \text{收敛}, & \alpha = 1 且\ \beta > 1 \\[4pt] 
> \text{发散}, & \text{其余情况} 
> \end{cases}, \\[8pt]
> &②\int_{a}^{+\infty} \frac{1}{x^\alpha \ln^\beta x} \,\mathrm{d}x 
> \begin{cases} 
> \text{收敛}, & \alpha > 1 \\[4pt] 
> \text{收敛}, & \alpha = 1 且\ \beta > 1 \\[4pt] 
> \text{发散}, & \text{其余情况} 
> \end{cases}
> \end{aligned}
> $$


> [!tip]+ 常用结论
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> &①\int_{0}^{1}\frac{1}{（x-a）^{p}}dx\left\{\begin{array}{cc}0<p<1,&\text{收敛}\\p\geq1&\text{发散,}\end{array}\right.\\
> &②\int_{1}^{+\infty}\frac{1}{(b-x)^{p}}dx\left\{\begin{array}{cc}p<1,&\text{收敛}\\p\geq1&\text{发散}\end{array}\right.
> \end{aligned}$$
