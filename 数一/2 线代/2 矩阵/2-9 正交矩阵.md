## 定义

若 $n$ 阶矩阵 $A$ 满足 $A^T A = AA^T = E$，则称矩阵 $A$ 为**正交矩阵**。

## 性质

$$Q^T Q = E \Rightarrow\begin{cases}|Q| = \pm 1 \\Q^{-1} = Q^T \\Q \text{ 由规范正交基组成}\end{cases}\quad\begin{cases}Q^T, Q^*, Q^{-1} \text{ 也是正交矩阵} \\\text{若干个同阶正交阵相乘仍是正交阵} \\\text{特征值 } \lambda = 1 \text{ 或 } -1\\ \text{行 (列) 向量都是单位向量且两两正交（坐标平方和=1）}\end{cases}$$

## 联想

设 $A = \begin{bmatrix} a_1 & b_1 & c_1 \\ a_2 & b_2 & c_2 \\ a_3 & b_3 & c_3 \end{bmatrix}$ 是正交矩阵
则
$$A^T A = \begin{bmatrix} a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\ b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \\ c_1 & c_2 & c_3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} a_1 & b_1 & c_1 \\ a_2 & b_2 & c_2 \\ a_3 & b_3 & c_3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$
从而有
$$\begin{align*}a_1^2 + a_2^2 + a_3^2 &= 1 \qquad \text{单位} \\a_1 b_1 + a_2 b_2 + a_3 b_3 &= 0 \qquad \text{正交} \\a_1 c_1 + a_2 c_2 + a_3 c_3 &= 0\end{align*}$$


$$
A^T A = \begin{bmatrix} \alpha_1^T \\ \alpha_2^T \\ \alpha_3^T \end{bmatrix} [\alpha_1, \alpha_2, \alpha_3]
$$

$$
= \begin{bmatrix} 
\alpha_1^T\alpha_1 & \alpha_1^T\alpha_2 & \alpha_1^T\alpha_3 \\ 
\alpha_2^T\alpha_1 & \alpha_2^T\alpha_2 & \alpha_2^T\alpha_3 \\ 
\alpha_3^T\alpha_1 & \alpha_3^T\alpha_2 & \alpha_3^T\alpha_3 
\end{bmatrix} 
= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
$$
